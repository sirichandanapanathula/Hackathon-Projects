# Google Dev Sprint Hackathon 2025

## Project Title
AI-Assisted Symptom Analysis and Medical Guidance Platform

## Overview
This project is a web-based platform designed to provide users with a secondary medical opinion based on the symptoms they enter. The goal of the platform is to assist users in better understanding possible health conditions related to their symptoms after they have already consulted a medical professional.

The system analyzes user-input symptoms and suggests possible diseases along with general medication recommendations that may be relevant to those conditions.

⚠️ Note: The platform is intended only as a secondary reference tool and does not replace professional medical advice.

## Problem Statement
Many individuals struggle to better understand their medical symptoms and diagnoses after visiting a doctor. There is often a need for a simple platform that allows users to input symptoms and receive structured information about potential diseases and general treatments.

## Solution
We developed a web application that:
- Allows users to log in securely
- Enables users to enter health symptoms
- Processes the symptoms to identify possible diseases
- Suggests relevant medicines and guidance based on the predicted condition
- Provides users with an easy-to-understand interface for health information

## Features
